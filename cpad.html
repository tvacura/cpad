Maldives

The Maldives are a tropical paradise with snow-white sand beaches, coconut groves,

the purest azure sea. Untouched, pristine nature of South Asia, amazing underwater

world, anticipation of delight, tranquility and heavenly pleasure - this is what can

characterize a holiday in the Maldives.

Bali

Bali is famous for tourism because it was, in part, “discovered” by the tourism industry

before the rest of Indonesia, and then developed a culture that actively welcomes

tourists. Natural beauty, stunning beaches, ancient temples, etc. Bali has a vibrant

nightlife, with beach clubs, bars and nightclubs.
